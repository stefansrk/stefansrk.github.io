Teaching

As an Associate Fellow of the UK Higher Education Academy and as one of the Graduate Teaching Assistants in the Department of Informatics at King's College London, from 2016 to 2020, I used to be involved in the following undergraduate and postgraduate modules:

7CCSMAIN/6CCS3AIN Artificial Intelligence

7CCSMEAI Philosophy & Ethics of Artificial Intelligence

7CCSMML Machine Learning

5CCS2ELA/4CCS1ELA Elementary Logic with Applications

4CCS1IAI Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

As a special mention, I have also held guest lectures for 6CCS3AIN Artificial Intelligence and the 7CCSMART Advanced Research Topics in Computer Science modules.

Most importantly, I think students enjoy the way I teach and interact with them. Probably that's why they've nominated me for the 2020 King's Education Awards, and previously, for the 2018/2019 Outstanding Teaching Assistant Awards in the Department of Informatics.

Here is some of the feedback that got me nominated for the teaching awards: "Stefan works extremely hard to make tutorials engaging by introducing games, interactive polls, and even extremely creative and funny scenarios. This made me excited to attend each and every one of his tutorials! Stefan has also been an extremely supportive TA, both inside and outside tutorials and labs."
